About Us

It is situated at the heart of Calauan, Laguna. Our barangay boasts of a rich culture, long history, and community spirit. At Barangay Lamot, we are proud to give our people and visitors the feeling of belongingness.

Our Mission

Our goal is to uplift everyone's quality of life through socioeconomic development, environmental sustainability, and community well-being. In general, we exert efforts in providing required services to meet various demands in our neighborhood.

Community Engagement

We are big advocates of involving the community. Our personnel actively participate in cultural activities, local governance, and other efforts helping our barangay, in general, be a healthy, safe, and energetic environment for everybody.
