Greetings!

Welcome to Barangay Lamot, is dedicated to serving our community with a range of essential services aimed at ensuring the well-being of all residents.

Community and Family Values

We have a very strong feeling of community in Barangay Lamot 2. It's our dream to make a community wherein neighbors help neighbors and where families can really thrive. Our barangay is full of projects that involve community services, health services, and youth educational assistance among others-all for the betterment of the people here.


Join Us!

We invite you warmly to visit Barangay Lamot and see the warmth of our neighborhood. Whether you are a long-time resident or just setting foot here, you will surely feel at home. Let us continuously strive together for a green, lively, and inviting community that respects our heritage, cares for our families, and embraces the future.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to welcoming you to our barangay!
