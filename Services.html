Our Services

Here, in Barangay Lamot, a wide scope of services has been developed and initiated for the improvement of standards of living of our people. Main services included are:

  • Educational Support: Initiatives created to provide scholarships and instructional materials to kids.
  • Health Services: Access to wellness initiatives and neighborhood health clinics to encourage healthy living.
  • Public Safety: Together with local law enforcement to guarantee our residents' safety and security.
  • Community Events: Recreational and cultural activities that everyone can do all the time which further develop the sense of belonging and connectedness to the community.
  • Waste Management: Recycling programs and sustainable garbage disposal programs are vital in keeping our neighborhood clean and tidy.
